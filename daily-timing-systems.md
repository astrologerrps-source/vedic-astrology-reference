## Hora (planetary hours)
First hora = weekday lord; sequence repeats **Sun ‚Üí Venus ‚Üí Mercury ‚Üí Moon ‚Üí Saturn ‚Üí Jupiter ‚Üí Mars**. Day horas = daytime/12 (unequal with night except at equinoxes).

## Abhijit Muhurat
~48 minutes centred on **local solar noon** (midpoint of sunrise/sunset). Observed daily **except Wednesday**; not used for marriage in most traditions.

## Dishashool (direction avoided at journey start)
East: Mon, Sat ¬∑ West: Sun, Fri ¬∑ North: Tue, Wed ¬∑ South: Thu. Applies to outward commencement only, not the return.
